# Format Status

**Document Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-28

This document describes the current publication status of the **Draphera HP‑GL/HP‑GL/HP‑GL/2 Bible** and its associated specifications.  
The status model is inspired by established technical‑standard organizations such as W3C, ISO, and ECMA.

---

## Status Levels

### 1. Draft
A preliminary version of the document.  
Content may change significantly.  
Feedback and contributions are encouraged.

### 2. Candidate Standard
The document is considered stable.  
Implementations are expected to validate the specification.  
Only clarifications and minor adjustments are allowed.

### 3. Recommended Standard
The document is mature and widely validated.  
Changes are limited to corrections and editorial improvements.  
Breaking changes require a new major version.

### 4. Deprecated
The document or section is no longer recommended for use.  
It remains available for historical reference.

### 5. Obsolete
The document or section has been superseded and is no longer maintained.

## Versioning and Status Relationship
- **MAJOR** version increments may change the status of entire sections.  
- **MINOR** versions may promote sections from Draft → Candidate.  
- **PATCH** versions never change status levels.
